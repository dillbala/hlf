Mission

Huma Lung Foundation(HLF) is a project of Human Cares, a Corporate Social Responsibility(CSR) intiative of huma Specialist Hospital & Research Centre Pvt.Ltd.

Huma Lung Foundatinon is an Effort in response to the epidemic of lung disease, which kills thousands of people worldwide. The key area of interest at the program are air pollution, tuberculosis, repiratory infections and chronic lung diseases. HLF provides technical and research support to goverment and non-govermental organizations.
